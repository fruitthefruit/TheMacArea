Food-wise, strawberries are a favorite, and I prefer clean, calm environments where I can focus or just chill. These interests reflect my laid-back personality and my need for balance between rest and activity.

I may not be overly outgoing all the time, but I value genuine friendships and shared experiences. Being around others helps me learn, grow, and enjoy life more.
